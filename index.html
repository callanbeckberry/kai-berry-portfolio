Therefore | 2019

Cloud Storage for thinkers and collaborators

Therefore is a web-app cloud storage solution focusing on file sharing and collaboration.

Upload, create, and edit files all from the cloud, and share with others for live multi-user editing.

Busybus | 2019

Taking the guesswork out of grabbing the bus

BusyBus is an iOS transit app solving overcrowded bus stops in major metropolitan areas.

Look at bus stops on a map, add favorite busses, and see how long until busses arrive.
