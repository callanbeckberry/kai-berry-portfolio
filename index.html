Design Process

Let's make your ideas reality

Research

Assess problem and scope of issue, conduct surveys and interviews to define userbase, analyze competition, and define MVP

Define

Develop visual branding, and utilize user base to find preferences to leverage and build/test high fidelity mockups

Refine

Explore solutions by utilizing user stores, personas, flows, and MVP to create wireframes and refine informational architecture

Develop

Create interactive prototypes, developting responsively and thinking accessibly while conducting further tests and analysis
